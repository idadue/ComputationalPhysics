\begin{abstract}\label{sec:abstract}
We develop a Monte Carlo simulation of the spread of an infectious disease. The aim of creating such a simulation is to investigate how a disease spreads throughout a given population over time. As a learning exercise, we include a deterministic approach using the fourth-order Rung-Kutta method.
